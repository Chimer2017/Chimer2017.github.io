De-risking Blue Carbon Makes it Sexy for Everyone

Blue carbon projects have a number of advantages over terrestrial afforestation and conservation projects. The potential for carbon capture is far greater for the same surface areas. Land environments benefit from the protection from coastal storms. Ocean environments benefit from the protection against land erosions and pollutants. There are many additional environmental benefits to mangrove and seagrass cultivation.

Blue carbon projects also come with a number of additional challenges, particularly for purchasers of carbon credits and blue carbon developers who wish to sell those credits in order to fund their vital projects.

Itâ€™s more difficult to measure the carbon content of submerged soils. Seagrass quantity and quality is harder to assess in murky waters. Causes and extents of seagrass degradation can be less obvious than deforestation and other environmental damages on land. Quantifying the health and carbon capture rates or blue carbon ecosystems is an understudied area. Even where models exist, they may not be well known to the people on the ground working on blue carbon projects.

For all these reasons, we feel that there will be substantial value in developing a standardized process for measuring, assessing, and certifying blue carbon projects. Accomplishing this will open up considerable funding opportunities for blue carbon project developers. This will greatly accelerate blue carbon initiatives around the world.

Develop A Plan

Every successful project has a good plan. At CCS, we are developing powerful environmental models pairing ecological data with human pressures to map out and understand where projects may have most success. We pair this with rigorous business models to then establish economics projections.

Analyze Potential

Using machine learning, remote sensing, and state of the art measurement methods, we aim to reliably predict the potential carbon curve and economic productivity of a blue carbon project, providing clear insights and key metrics for all stakeholders involved.

Connect with Capital

Blue carbon projects are only half the battle. Often those looking to invest in carbon offset projects and those working on blue carbon projects do not roam in the same circles. We connect the two groups, regardless of where they live.
